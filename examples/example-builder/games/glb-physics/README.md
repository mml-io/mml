# GLB Physics Demo

A simple demonstration of physics with GLB models.

## How it works

- Click the funnel to spawn a duck model above it
- The duck falls due to gravity and collides with the ground and funnel
- Ducks are automatically cleaned up after 10 seconds

## Features

- GLB models with physics colliders
- Dynamic rigidbodies (ducks fall)
- Kinematic rigidbodies (funnel and ground stay in place)
- Click interaction to spawn objects
